Under-the-Sea Salad (Jell-O Recipe Book)

Ingredients
    • 1 package (3 oz.) Jell-O Lime
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1 can (1 lb.) pear halves
    • 2 packages (3 oz. each) cream cheese
    • 1 cup boiling water
    • 1 tablespon lemon juice
    • 1/8 teaspoon ginger

Instructions
    1. Dissolve Jell-O and salt in boiling water.
    2. Drain pears, measuring 3/4 cup syrup; add water if necessary.
    3. Dice pears and set aside.
    4. Add pear syrup and lemon juice to gelatin.
    5. Measure 1 1/4 cups (Jell-O) into a 1-quart mold.
    6. Chill until set, but not firm.
    7. Meanwhile, soften chesse until creamy.
    8. Gradually add remaining gelatin, blending until smooth.
    9. Add ginger.
    10. Chill until very thick.
    11. Fold in pears.
    12. Spoon into mold.
    13. Chill until firm.
    14. Unmold on crisp lettuce.
    15. Makes about 4 cups, or 8 salads.
